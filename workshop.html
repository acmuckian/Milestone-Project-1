Available Courses

Find more information about our courses below, including duration, what you will learn and prices.

Introduction to Woodworking

Perfect for beginners, this two day course introduces you to hand tooling techniques. You will learn how to saw a straight line, use tools safely in the workshop, measuring and marking, tool sharpening and much more.

  • Next available date: 30th November 2024 on request
  • Duration: 2 days
  • Cost: €170 pp
Build a Birdhouse

Come to build a beautiful birdhouse in this course. You will be supplied with the necessary equipment, such as protective clothing, tools and wood. You will be able to bring your own birdhouse at the end of this course.

  • Next available date: 1st December 2024 on request
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Cost: €95 pp
Fundamentals of Woodturning

Learn how to work with the lathe safely in this fun two day course, and make an item to bring home. You will learn about lathe tools, what to look out for when buying a lathe, lathe mounting options and finishing techniques. There will be focus on the bowl gouge. At the end of the course you will have your very own bowl to bring home.

  • Next Available Date: 14th December 2024 on request
  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Cost: €110 pp
Cabinetmaking: Dovetails

Learn how to excel in dovetail joinery in this course, where you will be taught about marking out both through and half-blind dovetails, erasing any gaps or mistakes and the traditional german layout method to dovetails which is popular in the cabinetmaking industry.

  • Next available date: 9th November 2024 on request
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Cost: €70 pp
